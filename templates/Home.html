What is Positive Behavior Support (PBS) and how does it benefit individuals?

Positive Behavior Support (PBS) is a person-centered approach designed to improve the quality of life by addressing challenging behaviors through non-aversive methods. PBS focuses on understanding the root causes of behaviors and implementing strategies that enhance overall well-being and reduce distress.
