Our Services

We provide specialized services to support individuals with complex needs, focusing on promoting independence, positive behavior, and community engagement.

Positive Behavior Support (PBS)

Evidence-based strategies to improve quality of life by promoting positive behavior and reducing challenging behaviors.

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

Tailored support for individuals to live independently, focusing on daily living skills, community involvement, and personal care.

Early Intervention Programs (EIP)

Specialized programs addressing developmental delays, focusing on enhancing cognitive, social, and emotional growth.

Short-Term Accommodation (STA)

Provides a safe, supportive environment for short-term stays, offering a break for caregivers and continuity of quality care.

Community Participation (CP)

Helping individuals engage in community activities, develop social skills, and build connections to promote inclusion and belonging.

Health & Wellness Programs (HWP)

Holistic programs focused on improving physical and mental well-being through fitness, nutrition, and emotional support.

What types of support does Illawarra Disability Care offer?

At Illawarra Disability Care, we provide a range of support services including personalized support plans, comprehensive behavioral support, communication assistance, collaborative care, and supported living solutions. We also offer transition assistance for individuals moving from assessment and treatment units to independent or supported living environments.

What is Positive Behavior Support (PBS) and how does it benefit individuals?

Positive Behavior Support (PBS) is a person-centered approach designed to improve the quality of life by addressing challenging behaviors through non-aversive methods. PBS focuses on understanding the root causes of behaviors and implementing strategies that enhance overall well-being and reduce distress.

How does Illawarra Disability Care assist with communication challenges?

We offer a range of alternative communication methods and tools to support individuals with verbal communication difficulties. This includes providing assistive technologies, training individuals and their families on using communication aids, and facilitating effective expression to ensure that needs are clearly communicated and understood.
